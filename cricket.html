CRICKET

Over the last few years, the craze for cricket in India has increased manifold. Youths from all walks of life are showing interests to make their career in it. In India, cricket is considered not just a sport, but a valued religion. To cater to the increasing demand of the sifting scenario, Sports Shala has taken it upon to itself to promote excellence in budding cricket aspirants. And with that in mind, it offers cutting-edge cricket pathways by integrating a well-crafted cricket training program.

As a kid grows mentally, so should his or her cricket skills. At JSR we build up a road on which candidates can attend the right cricket coaching in Gurgaon and develop their latent potential. Besides providing world-class cricket training facilities, we also give emphasis on tournament preparation as well. All this is done just to coach aspirants and make them renowned cricket players of future India.
